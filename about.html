Aims & Objectives

Aims of the Institute are: - i) To ensure a steady flow of skilled workers in different trades for the industry, ii) To raise the quality and quantity of industrial production by systematic training of workers iii) To reduce the unemployment among the educated youth by equipping them for suitable industrial employment.

Curriculum

The curriculum of each trade is uniformly sub-divided into activities and tasks to be carried out every week. The curriculum of most of the trades has been revised and updated by the trade wise committees constituted by the Directorate General of Training (DGT). The curriculum also shows the achievement level at the end of each unit.

Training Frame Work

(a) Technical Training in Engineering trades such as Electrician, Fitter for 2 years & Welder for 1-year duration. (b) Training in Non-Engineering Trades such as Computer Operator and Programming Assistant for one-year duration.

About us

Inspired by the ideals of renunciation and service, Ramakrishna Mission has been engaged in different forms of social service activities for more than a century across the world. They have served millions of men, children and women irrespective of their caste, religion or race as they see the living God in them. Ramakrishna Mission Ashrama, Asansol is a branch centre of Ramakrishna Mission, Belur Math, Howrah. It is located in Asansol, P.O. R.K Mission, Dt. Paschim Bardhamam, Pin- 7113305, West Bengal. It has emerged as the vocational training centre since 2003. Hundreds of youth who were unable to go for higher education have been trained in skills and are gainfully employed in various well-known industries. Another wing of VTC is introduced as industrial training institute (ITI) in 2019.


Ram Krishna Mission PVT ITI Asansol imparts the training in technological and industrial skills with four Trade courses - Electrician, Fitter, Welder (Gas & Electric) and COPA (computer operator and programming assistant) - under Craftsman Training Scheme (C.T.S.) affiliated to National Council for Vocational Training (N.C.V.T), New Delhi. Here, we provide the quality and balanced technical education facilitated with practical and industrial exposure for the gainful employment to the students. We help them develop communication skills, appropriate social and leisure skills, vocational skills and behaviour. This institution has been flourished with Swami Vivekananda's ideas to build the bridge for the masses, particularly rural India, to connect to modern industry. At Ram Krishna Mission PVT ITI Asansol, we believe that each child deserves the right to lead a fruitful life. We are committed to build the students productive and competent citizens of the knowledge economy. Our vision is to becomethe pioneer in imparting Vocational Training and Education and the preferred destination for ITI courses.

National Skill Development Corporation(NSDC)

National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) is a not-for-profit public limited company incorporated on July 31, 2008 under section 25 of the Companies Act, 1956 (corresponding to section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013). NSDC was set up by Ministry of Finance as Public Private Partnership (PPP) model. The Government of India through Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (MSDE) holds 49% of the share capital of NSDC, while the private sector has the balance 51% of the share capital.
